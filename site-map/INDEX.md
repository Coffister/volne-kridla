## Site Purpose

Voľné Krídla ("Free Wings") is a Slovak parrot free-flight training school run by **Franka** (Instagram: @volne.kridla). The business offers:

- **Online konzultácia** (Online consultation) — video call training advice
- **Osobná konzultácia** (In-person consultation) — on-site training sessions
- **Kurz voľného lietania** (Free-flight course) — structured multi-session course
